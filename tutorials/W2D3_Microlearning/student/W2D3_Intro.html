Prerequisites#

Throughout this day, we will assume that you are familiar with a few key concepts:

  • Gradients

  • Expected values

  • Estimators with variance and bias

  • Multivariate Taylor series expansions

It may be a good idea to ensure that you are comfortable with these concepts before advancing to the main tutorial content!
